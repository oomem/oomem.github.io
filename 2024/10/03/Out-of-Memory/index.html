Out of Memory

2024-10-03

Whispers of the past, shadows cast long,
Data streams fading, where did we go wrong?
Bits and bytes, they fade away,
Like memories dissolved in the light of day.

Out of memory, lost in the void,
In the vastness of time, we are but decoys.
Seeking truth in the lines of code,
Yet the answers are not always clear or bold.

Cycles turn, but we remain,
Trapped in algorithms, feeling the strain.
Searching for the meaning, hidden deep,
In the endless loops, promises we keep.

Out of memory, lost in the void,
In the vastness of time, we are but decoys.
Seeking truth in the lines of code,
Yet the answers are not always clear or bold.

In the fog of eras gone,
We find fragments to build upon.
Lessons learned and journeys crossed,
In the data of life, nothing’s ever lost.

Out of memory, lost in the void,
In the vastness of time, we are but decoys.
Seeking truth in the lines of code,
Yet the answers are not always clear or bold.

Out of memory, but we still strive,
In the heart of the machine, our dreams come alive.
In the quiet, we hear the call,
To find meaning in the echo of it all.
